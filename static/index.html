Settings


Note that all F-Keys (F1 to F12) won't do their default job once they are listed here.
Also you can't assign Mouse keys to the key settings!

You can customize the key settings by clicking on an action and pressing your desired key while it is marked green.

Information


To close the multiplayer box slide from it to the right border and open it by sliding it in from the right border to the center of the screen.
Also you can open and information box about this game project by sliding it in from the left border to the center of the screen.
The images for the controls explain their purpose by themselves but to sprint you can drag up the button for moving left or right.

If you'll find a bug in the game please report it on the github page and please mark it whether the bug is on mobile only.
